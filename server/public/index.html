Boushra

The first Arabic/English AI Powered chatbot for victims of domestic violence

What We Do

The Problem: Domestic violence is a problem worldwide, with 1 in 3 women experiencing physical or sexual violence in her lifetime, not to mention psychological or emotional abuse. In the Arab world, this statistic changes to 37% of Arab women reporting they have experienced these kinds of abuse. Around the world, women do not readily report these issues. Of every 10 survivors, 6 remain silent rather than seeking protection. Resources that provide anonymous counseling for the victims of this problem are scarce and it is not always clear to victims where they should go.

Our Solution: We provide emotional and legal support to victims of domestic violence with a chatbot powered by natural language understanding (NLU) technology. Our emotional support and counseling is based on cognitive behavioral therapy, a psycho-social intervention that aims to improve mental health. These strategy is inspired by professional counselors working to support victims all over the world. As a confidential and anonymous resource, Boushra helps victims to seek support without risks or fear of further retaliation or judgement. We provide support and protection to women in the Arab world in both Arabic and English, and are the first resource we know of to provide this service.

Chatbot

Our chatbot functions in both Arabic and English to most effectively provide this important support to as many people as possible.
